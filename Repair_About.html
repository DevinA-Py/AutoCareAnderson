Our Story

Anderson Auto Care started as a small two-bay garage with a simple goal: treat every customer’s car as if it were our own. Over the years we’ve grown, but that mindset hasn’t changed. We believe in clear communication, honest recommendations, and repairs that you can rely on.

Whether you drive an older commuter car, a family SUV, or a work truck, our team is here to help you stay safe on the road without breaking your budget.

Inside the Shop

Our shop is equipped with modern diagnostic tools and equipment, but we still rely on experience and common sense. From check engine lights to strange noises, we take time to properly diagnose the problem before recommending any work.

Our Promise to You

We know that auto repair can be stressful, especially when you’re not sure what’s wrong with your vehicle. That’s why we promise to:

  • Explain repairs in plain language.
  • Get your approval before doing any work.
  • Use quality parts that meet or exceed OEM standards.
  • Stand behind our work with a fair warranty.

If you ever have a question about a repair or estimate, just ask. We’re here to help—not pressure you.
